Gerard's Family Page

Just a little information about my family here.

My dad, Gerald, is chairman of the Department of Environmental Health at the University of Washington. My mom, Johanna, is a doctor at the Hall Health Center on campus.

I have two brothers and two sisters; one older and one younger of each. My older sister, Loeske, is a young entrepreneur in Oak Harbor, WA, along with her husband, Bud and son, Travis. My older brother, William, is a carpetlayer in the Seattle area; his daughter Alex and son Austin have the lock on 'cute'. My younger sister, Christine, is a college instructor in Portland, OR, where her husband, Jim, works at Intel. My younger brother, Louis, is a freshman at Dordt College in Souix Center, IA. He actually has his own web page, in addition to a unique sense of humor.

Hopefully soon I'll get some pictures of everyone online, for the moment, I merely have this (somewhat ancient!) photo of me with all my siblings. A little nutty but my feeling is that it rather sums up the flavor of familty get-togethers.
