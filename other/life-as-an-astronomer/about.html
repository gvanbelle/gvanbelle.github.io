Yes, I know, in this photograph I look about 16. I'm a little older than that. It is indeed me upon arrival at the Department of Physics & Astronomy at the University of Wyoming in the fall of 1993, so the photo is a tad dated - depends on how you look at it, really. A few years or a Ph.D. - that'll put lines in your face and grey in your hair. The simple truth of the matter is that the picture was merely overexposed a touch. For other photos of me and my friends, try my photo album. Really though, it's not that important what I look like.
